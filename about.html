About Precious Metals

Precious metals such as gold (Au), silver (Ag), platinum (Pt), and palladium (Pd) have been valued for thousands of years for their beauty, rarity, and industrial uses.

  • Gold: Jewelry, investment, electronics, and as a store of value.
  • Silver: Industrial uses, jewelry, and currency.
  • Platinum: Automotive catalytic converters, jewelry, medical devices.
  • Palladium: Electronics, automotive, dentistry.
